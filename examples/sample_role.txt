Talent Strategist — Palantir Technologies (New York)

Palantir builds software that empowers organizations to effectively integrate their data, decisions, and operations. We're seeking a Talent Strategist to join our Talent team and help identify, attract, and close exceptional candidates for critical roles across the organization.

Responsibilities:
- Own full-cycle recruiting for technical and business roles across engineering, deployment, and go-to-market teams
- Partner with hiring managers and leadership to define role requirements, calibrate candidate profiles, and build pipeline strategies
- Source and engage passive candidates through creative outreach and relationship-building
- Manage the candidate experience end-to-end, from first touch through offer close
- Analyze pipeline metrics and adjust sourcing strategies based on data
- Contribute to talent brand and employer positioning initiatives
- Operate with autonomy and a bias toward action in a fast-paced, mission-driven environment

Requirements:
- 2+ years of experience in recruiting, talent strategy, or a related operational role
- Demonstrated ability to source and close candidates in competitive markets
- Strong analytical skills — comfort with data, pipeline metrics, and forecasting
- Excellent written and verbal communication
- Track record of building relationships with senior stakeholders
- High agency, self-direction, and comfort with ambiguity
- Interest in defense, technology, and Palantir's mission

Preferred:
- Experience recruiting for technical or enterprise software roles
- Prior experience at a high-growth technology company
- Background in operations, consulting, or strategy
